General Rules

  • Always begin with: "Buying" - "Selling" - "Trading" - "Selling or trading"
  • For "Buying" put "Budget:". For "Selling" put "Price:".
  • If no Budget/Price mentioned, use "Negotiable"
  • First letter is ALWAYS capital, end with a period (unless ending with a number)
  • Use $ before the amount, not after ($5 Million, $500.000)
  • Use full stop (.) instead of comma (,) for prices
  • Do NOT use "k" for thousand or "M" for Million - write out in numbers

Term Replacements

  • Max config/max tuning → with full configuration
  • Nearly max/part lvl3 → with partial configuration
  • Body upgrades → with visual upgrades
  • Turbo → turbo kit
  • Drift tuning → drift kit
  • Luminous rims → luminous wheels
  • Level 1/low level → low quality
  • Level 2/medium level → medium quality
  • Level 3/high level → high quality
  • Crates/cases → containers
  • Spray cans → paint cans
  • Extras → of type

Auto Category Specific

  • Maximum ONE vehicle per ad (unless trading)
  • Vehicle names in quotes: "Brand Model"
  • Order of features: configuration → visual upgrades → luminous wheels → insurance → turbo kit → drift kit
  • Can only trade Vehicle for Vehicle

Real Estate Specific

  • Maximum one numbered property, or up to 3 unnumbered
  • Use № for numbered properties
  • Garage spaces: 2 g.s., 5 g.s., 9 g.s., 25 g.s.
  • Warehouse: w.h.
  • Features order: garden → garage → warehouse → custom interior → insurance → helipad → others → location

Prohibited Content

  • Firearms, ammunition, armor
  • Drugs, weed/cannabis
  • EMS/surgical masks, balaclava masks
  • Lock picks, crowbars, ropes
  • Grand coins, Battlepass items
  • Specific family names
  • Gangs, nationality
  • Sexual content, sale of people
  • State leaders (unless in leader-list)
